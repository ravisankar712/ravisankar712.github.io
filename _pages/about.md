Hello World! I am a PhD student (2021 – present) at the University of Virginia, working with professor Diana Vaman. 

Broadly speaking, my research is about Quantum Field Theory, and its relation with classical and quantum gravity. With my advisor and other collaborators, I have been working on extracting classical observables from quantum scattering, aspects of quantum entanglement in conformal field theories and holography. You can read more about it in the [Research page]((https://ravisankar712.github.io/publications/)). 

I am also passionate about teaching physics. During my PhD years, I have taught classes on some advanced topics in Quantum Field Theory – namely QFT with path integrals, bosonization, and Wilsonian RG and Critical phenomena etc. Some of these lectures are on YouTube (thanks to my awesome classmates!). See the [teaching page]((https://ravisankar712.github.io/teaching/)) for the videos and lecture notes.
